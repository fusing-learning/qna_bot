Finance Policy Document

1. Expense Reimbursements
- All claims must be submitted within 30 days of expense.
- Receipts must be attached for all items above $20.

2. Budget Management
- Department heads are responsible for their allocated budgets.
- Quarterly budget reviews are conducted with the Finance team.

3. Procurement Guidelines
- All purchases above $5,000 must be approved by the CFO.
- Use preferred vendors where applicable.

4. Travel Policy
- Economy class travel for flights under 6 hours.
- Hotel bookings must be within approved corporate rates.

5. Financial Reporting
- Monthly reports due by the 5th working day of each month.
- Financial irregularities must be reported immediately to the CFO.
